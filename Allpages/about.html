How can we help you?

Find answers to common questions about our wedding planning services. If you need further assistance, please feel free to contact us.

At WhiteAura, we offer a comprehensive range of wedding planning services, including:

  • Full-service wedding planning
  • Day-of coordination
  • Venue selection and booking
  • Decor and floral design
  • Catering and menu planning
  • Entertainment and music arrangement
  • Photography and videography services
  • Custom wedding invitations and stationery

We recommend booking our services at least 6-12 months in advance. This allows ample time for thorough planning and ensures that we can secure the best vendors and venues for your special day.

Our pricing structure varies based on the scope of services required. We offer customizable packages to suit different needs and budgets. For detailed pricing information, please contact us directly for a consultation.
