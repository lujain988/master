To create a new event, navigate to the "Manage Events" section in the sidebar. Click on the "Add Event" button and fill in the required details.

You can manage clients by navigating to the "Manage Clients" section in the sidebar. Here you can add, edit, or delete client information.

To upload photos, go to the relevant section (Events, Clients, Vendors, etc.), find the item you want to add a photo to, and use the provided file upload form.
